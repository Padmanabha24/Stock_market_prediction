About LSTM

Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks excel at modeling temporal dependencies in stock data, capturing long-term trends and patterns for accurate predictions in volatile markets.

  • Strengths: Handles sequential data, captures long-term dependencies.
  • Use Case: Forecasting stock prices with recurring patterns.
  • Features: Includes RSI, moving averages, and volume trends.
